# 💄 Impact of Digital Marketing on Cosmetics Buying Behaviour

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes how digital marketing strategies influence consumer purchasing decisions in the cosmetics industry. The study focuses on how online platforms shape customer preferences and buying behaviour.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* To understand the impact of social media on buying behaviour
* To analyze the role of influencers in product decisions
* To study the importance of online reviews and ratings
* To examine how discounts and ads affect purchase decisions

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Tableau

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Social media platforms strongly influence cosmetics purchases
* Influencer marketing builds trust and affects decision-making
* Online reviews and ratings play a major role in product selection
* Discounts and digital ads increase purchase intention
